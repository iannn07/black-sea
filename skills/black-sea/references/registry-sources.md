# Registry & Source Cheat-Sheet

Map the record you need to the specific public source that holds it. Query the **primary registry**
before any secondary summary. Availability, cost, and coverage vary by jurisdiction — where a source
is paywalled or in-person-only, that's a Collection Gap, not a guess.

## Corporate registration & filings

- **Aggregator (start here):** OpenCorporates (cross-jurisdiction company/officer graph).
- **US:** SEC EDGAR (public-company filings), Secretary of State registries (per state).
- **UK:** Companies House (free full filings + officers + PSC/beneficial owners).
- **EU:** BRIS / e-Justice Business Registers portal; national registers (e.g. DE Unternehmensregister).
- **Indonesia:** AHU Online (Ditjen AHU, Kemenkumham) for PT/legal-entity data; OSS (Online Single
  Submission) for business licensing/NIB.
- **Global-South & offshore:** national registries where public; ICIJ Offshore Leaks DB for
  *previously-reported* offshore links (secondary — grade accordingly).

## Beneficial ownership / control

- UK PSC register; EU UBO registers (access varies post-2022 rulings); US FinCEN BOI (restricted
  access — usually a gap). Cross-check with officer/address overlaps from OpenCorporates.

## Litigation, insolvency, liens

- **US:** PACER (federal, paywalled), CourtListener/RECAP (free mirror), state court portals, UCC
  filings per state.
- **UK:** The Gazette (insolvency/notices), Courts and Tribunals judgments.
- **Indonesia:** Direktori Putusan Mahkamah Agung (court decisions); SIPP for case tracking.

## Sanctions, PEP, debarment

- OFAC SDN & consolidated lists (US); EU consolidated sanctions; UN Security Council list; UK OFSI.
- World Bank / ADB debarment lists; national procurement blacklists.
- PEP: no single free authoritative list — triangulate official gazettes, disclosures, credible media.

## IP & innovation

- Patents: USPTO, EUIPO, Espacenet, WIPO PATENTSCOPE, Google Patents. Trademarks: USPTO TESS,
  EUIPO, WIPO Global Brand DB. **Indonesia:** PDKI (DJKI) for patents/marks.

## Regulatory & procurement

- Securities/financial regulators (SEC, UK FCA, **Indonesia OJK/IDX**). Procurement: SAM.gov (US),
  TED (EU), **LPSE / LKPP** (Indonesia). Import/export trade data where public.

## Web infrastructure (all public, all legitimate)

- WHOIS + domain history; DNS records; **crt.sh** certificate-transparency logs (subdomains, infra);
  Wayback Machine / archive.today (historical page states — a "new" claim that's years old is a finding).

## Digital exposure (the lawful "dark web" substitute)

- Have I Been Pwned (email/domain *appears* in known breaches) — the **fact and scale of exposure**
  only. Never ingest or reproduce breach contents; never build a person-dossier from leaked PII.

> Grade every source in the Evidence Register (Admiralty A–F / 1–6). An aggregator echoing one filing
> is one source, not many. Corroborate with independent origins before raising confidence.
